Common Questions

What is the deadline for filing taxes?

The general deadline for filing income tax returns in Bangladesh is usually November 30 of each year. However, it may be extended depending on government announcements.

What documents do I need to file my taxes?

You will need your TIN (Taxpayer Identification Number), proof of income (such as salary slips or business income records), bank statements, and investment documents. A detailed list will be provided when you start the filing process.

How much do you charge for tax filing services?

Our pricing depends on the type of service. For individual tax filing, the charge is BDT 3,000, while business tax filing starts from BDT 10,000. Please refer to our services page for more details.

Can I file taxes if I missed the deadline?

Yes, you can still file taxes after the deadline, but you may incur penalties and interest. We recommend filing as soon as possible to minimize these costs.

Do you offer tax consultation services?

Yes, we offer personalized tax consultations to help you navigate complex tax scenarios. Our consultation fees start from BDT 2,000 per hour.
